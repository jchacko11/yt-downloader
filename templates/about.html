Usage

To download a YouTube video, simply enter its link. Alternatively, replacing "www.youtube.com" with "yt.jchacko.dev" in the link will bring you to a download page.

For example this YouTube video:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IUyrlM1qkGU

Can be downloaded here:

https://yt.jchacko.dev/watch?v=IUyrlM1qkGU
